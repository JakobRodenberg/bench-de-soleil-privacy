What this app is

Bench de Soleil is provided free of charge, for personal use, as an informational aid. It estimates sun and shade from open map data and a weather forecast. It is not a navigation, safety, or emergency tool.

Bench, place, and sun/shade information is best-effort and comes from OpenStreetMap and public models. It can be incomplete, out of date, or wrong — a bench may not exist, or a sunny spot may be shaded in reality. Use your own judgement. The app is provided "as is," without warranties of any kind.
