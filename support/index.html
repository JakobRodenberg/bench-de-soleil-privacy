Support

Bench de Soleil maps public benches across Denmark by real sun and shade. This page is how to get help.

Contact

Questions, bug reports, or feedback: jakobrodenberg@gmail.com. It's a small, independent app — replies may take a little while.

Common questions

"Find nearest" or the blue dot does nothing

The app needs location permission, which it only asks for when you tap ↺ Locate or 🔭 Find nearest…. If you previously declined, enable it in iPhone Settings → Bench de Soleil → Location. The rest of the app works without it.

The map is grey / benches don't load

Usually a connectivity issue — check the phone has internet and isn't on a captive Wi-Fi sign-in page. The map recovers on its own when the connection returns.

A bench is in the wrong place, gone, or missing

Tap the bench (or long-press an empty spot) → Report a problem. Reports are crowdsourced: when enough people independently agree, the map corrects itself, and corrections are passed to OpenStreetMap.

How accurate is the sun/shade?

It's a best-effort estimate from building, tree, and terrain data plus a cloud forecast — not a guarantee. Use your own judgement; reality wins.
